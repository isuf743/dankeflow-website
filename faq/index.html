🏢 General

DankeFlow is a digital agency specializing in website design, development, SEO, and e-commerce solutions. We help businesses of all sizes build a strong online presence and grow through digital channels.

We are based in Tiranë, Albania, but we work with clients globally. All our communication and project management is done online, so location is never a barrier.

We work with startups, small and medium businesses, freelancers, and established companies across various industries — from retail and hospitality to tech, services, and e-commerce.

Yes, absolutely. We work with clients from across Europe, the US, and beyond. We communicate in English, Albanian, German, Italian, and Greek, and manage projects fully online with no time zone issues.

⚙️ Process

We start with a free discovery call to understand your goals. Then we send a detailed proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing. Once approved and the deposit is paid, we begin design, followed by development, testing, and launch.

A landing page takes 5–7 days. A full business website typically takes 2–4 weeks. E-commerce and custom platforms can take 4–8 weeks. We always agree on a clear timeline before starting.

💰 Pricing

Our plans start from $4,000/month for the Starter package. Pricing depends on the scope, complexity, and features required. Visit our Pricing page for details, or contact us for a custom quote.

No. Our pricing is fully transparent. The amount in your proposal is exactly what you pay. Any additional work outside the agreed scope is quoted separately and approved before it begins.

We accept bank transfers, PayPal, and other payment methods agreed upon in the project proposal. A 50% deposit is required before work begins, with the remaining balance due upon project completion.

Deposits are non-refundable once work has commenced, as they cover time and resources already allocated. If a project is cancelled before work begins, deposits may be partially refunded at our discretion.
